# Minoan Lexicon — Living Vocabulary

Four-tiered vocabulary organized by semantic domain. Tier 1 is attested; Tiers 2–4 are reconstructed. Every entry carries a confidence tag.

## Tier Key

| Tier | Confidence | Source |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **1** | CONFIRMED / PROBABLE | Gordon 1966 (60 readings) + di Mino 2026 (3 readings) |
| **2** | CANDIDATE | Corpus skeleton matched to Proto-Semitic root (d < 0.3) |
| **3** | SPECULATIVE | Analogical from Ugaritic/Phoenician/Akkadian roots |
| **4** | SPECULATIVE | Neologisms for composition (no corpus evidence) |
