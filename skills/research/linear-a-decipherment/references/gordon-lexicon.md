# Gordon's Semitic Lexicon for Linear A

- **YasharMana entries**: 3 readings from di Mino, *Thera, Knossos, and Minos* (2026)
- **Data file**: `data/gordon.json` (extracted from `semiticLexicon.js`)

## Yashar Mana Readings (3)

Tom di Mino's alternative readings for the libation formula, published in *Thera, Knossos, and Minos* (2026).

| Linear A | Reading | Semitic | Meaning | Note |
|----------|---------|---------|---------|------|
| JA-SA-SA-RA-MA-NA | ya-sha-sha-ra-ma-na | יָשָׁר מָנָה | righteous offering | Two words: √ישׁר + √מנה |
| A-SA-SA-RA-ME | a-sa-sa-ra-me | אַשָּׁר מֵא | offering from | Cf. Gordon's √שלם compound |
| JA-SA-SA-RA-ME | ya-sa-sa-ra-me | יָשָׁר מֵא | righteous offering | Prefixed variant |

**Key difference from Gordon**: Where Gordon reads a single compound from √שלם "to complete/pay," di Mino segments into two words: √ישׁר "straight, righteous" + √מנה "portion, offering."

## Normalization Rules

When looking up a word in the lexicon, the following normalization cascade applies:

1. **Exact match**: Look up the raw transliteration
2. **Subscript normalization**: PA₃ → PA3, KI₂ → KI2
3. **Logogram prefix stripping**: *301-TU → TU
4. **J/Y swap**: JA- ↔ YA- variants (GORILA uses JA-, Gordon often writes YA-)
